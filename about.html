About White Razor Productions

About the Company

White Razor Productions is the personally owned videography and principal photography company for a career film maker Ryan Murchison. It's the reflection of a lifelong inspiration in film. A company in pursuit of creating a cinema experience for projects close to the heart.Dedicated to quality production for every worthwhile experience needing a visual medium. As Ryan Murchison's Company operating with creative independence White Razor Productions has been contracted for principal photography, videography, short films, award winning documentaries, and local events.

About the Founder

Biography

Ryan Murchison has dedicated his life and career to video and cinema production. He has been making films since his teen years in public school. He has a passion for bringing stories to life. As a teen he aspired to direct and edit videos and films. In high school he began making films with paid actors and crew. Following highschool he left his home state going to university in Texas studying film. After which he continued traveling the states in search of more inspiration. Traveling to the West Coast and the Central Valley of California he branched out to News Broadcasting. His production work on the west coast eventually culminated into professional accalades and an international award winning documentary short centered around local California Politics. He would return to New Orleans to further his career as he continues to contract with film Productions and searches for inspiration for personal projects as well.
